Cities row The breakpoints for medium devices (with a screen width of 768px or greater) are defined within the .col-md- class, while the letters are specified in the .col-sm- class, intended for small devices (with a screen width of 576px or greater). Consequently, when we reduce the browser size, the Cities row is the first to undergo a layout adjustment.
